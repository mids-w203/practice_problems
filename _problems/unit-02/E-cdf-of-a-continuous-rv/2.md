---

Sol:
Given CDF, we can find its pdf as shown below.

$$\frac{d}{dx}[e^{\frac{-1}{x}}]=\frac{e^{frac{-1}{x}{x^2}}$$ 

and $\int_{0}^{\inf}\frac{e^{frac{-1}{x}{x^2}}dx=1$
