statement: |
    Suppose $X$ has density function $f(x)=x/2$ for $0<x<2$ and $0$ otherwise.
    Find 

    (a) the cumulative distribution function, 
     
    (b) $P(X<1)$, 

    (c) $P(X>3/2)$
---

(a) $\int_{-\infty}^x \frac{x}{2}dx = \int_{-\infty}^0 f(x)dx + \int_{0}^x f(x)dx = 0 + \int_{0}^{x}\frac{x}{2}dx = \frac{x^2}{4}$

(b) 1/4 
(x) 1-9/16 = 7/16
