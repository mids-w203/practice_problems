statement: | 
    Suppose $A$ and $B$ are disjoint with $P(A)=0.3$ and $P(B)=0.5$.
    What is $P(A'\cap B')$?
number: 17
answer: $0.2$
---
\\[
    \begin{align}
    P(A'\cap B')&=P((A\cup B)')\\\\\
    &=1-P(A\cup B)\\\\\
    &=1-\[P(A)+P(B)-P(A\cap B)\]\\\\\
    &=1-\[P(A)+P(B)-P(\emptyset)\]\\\\\
    &=1-\[0.3+0.5-0\]\\\\\
    &=0.2.
    \end{align}    
\\]
